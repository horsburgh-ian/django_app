Using MLR to Model Blood Pressure

Collaborators

Sara Colando

Descrption

As part of the Statistical Linear Model class I took in spring 2022, Sara and I used the NHANES dataset in R to model systemic blood pressure as a function of many variables. We compared models made using OLS to those with Ridge and Lasso penalties, as well as discussing variable selection, imputation, and finally touching on regression splines and kernal smoothing.

Analyzing Cheese Volitiles with GC-MS

Collaborators

Matt Teeter, Ryan Ruaysungnoen

Descrption

As part of the Advanced Analytical Chemistry class I'm taking in the fall of 2022, I designed as carried out an experiment to analyize and compare the volitile organic compounds in three types of cheeses deemed especially stinky by the local cheese shop. Most of the finding were expected, although we did find an 'acutely toxic' saturated hydrocarbon with high match quality in multiple runs of one cheese. Maybe think twice next time you're reaching for your Epoisses-Coup!.
